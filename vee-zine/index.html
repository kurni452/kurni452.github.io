This zine explores the different ideas and approaches on the word "activism" and how designers relate themselves with the word. This idea was originally inspired by the work Separated - Separados that we found during the MOMA PS-1 book fair last fall. The question "what is an artist role in the word activism?" is the main topic that we will mainly discuss in the provided interviews from artists that are also the contributors of Separated-Separados project.
